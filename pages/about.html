About Number Puzzle Pro

Number Puzzle Pro is a modern take on the classic 15-puzzle sliding game. Built as a single-page web app, it works offline and feels like a native app on phones, tablets and desktops.

Features

  • Five board sizes from 3×3 to 7×7 (8×8 and custom sizes ready in code).
  • Four target sequences: Classic, Upside Down, Spiral, Snake.
  • Number mode and Photo mode — turn any of your photos into a puzzle.
  • Hint, Undo, Redo, Pause, Restart, Shuffle.
  • Local high scores per board × sequence × mode.
  • Achievements, full statistics dashboard, and offline PWA support.
