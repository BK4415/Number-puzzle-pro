Privacy Policy

Number Puzzle Pro does not collect, transmit or share any personal data. The game runs entirely in your browser.

Local Storage

The app stores game settings, statistics, high scores and your last in-progress game in your browser's localStorage on your device. Uploaded photos for Photo Mode are processed locally and never leave your device. You can erase all stored data at any time from the Information panel.

Camera Access

If you choose the camera option for Photo Mode, your browser may ask for camera permission. The captured image stays on your device.
