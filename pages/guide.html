How To Play

  1. Choose a board size, sequence, mode and difficulty on the Home screen.
  2. For Photo Mode, upload an image (drag & drop, file picker, or camera).
  3. Press Play. The board shuffles into a solvable state.
  4. Tap or click any tile in the same row or column as the blank space — every tile between them slides toward the blank in one move.
  5. Re-arrange tiles to match the goal sequence (the live preview on Home shows the target).
  6. The timer starts on your first move. Try to solve in the fewest moves and shortest time.

Controls

  • Mouse / Touch: click a tile to slide it.
  • Keyboard: arrow keys move the blank, U undo, R redo, H hint, Space pause.
  • Swipe on Home: right → Info panel, left → Dashboard.

Tips

  • Solve the top row first, then work down.
  • On big boards, complete one row and one column at a time.
  • Use Hint sparingly — solving without hints unlocks an achievement.
